What is the Workshop?

The Workshop is your essential foundation for success at HEALTH HACK 2026. Scheduled for 16 February 2026, this intensive preparatory session bridges the gap between inspiration and implementation, equipping you with the knowledge, tools, and connections needed to excel during the main hackathon.


Through technical domain introduction sessions, you'll gain hands-on exposure to cutting-edge healthcare technology challenges. Our expert mentors from Johns Hopkins and industry leaders will provide clarity on hackathon themes and tracks, helping you understand the real-world context of problems you'll be solving.


This isn't just another lecture series. It's an interactive experience designed to help you form stronger ideas, build better prototypes, and develop winning strategies. By the end of the day, you'll have direct access to mentors, refined problem statements, and the confidence to create impactful healthcare innovations.

Practical Exposure & Hands-On Learning

The Workshop is designed to provide participants with meaningful, real-world exposure to healthcare problem-solving and innovation workflows. These sessions emphasize guided learning, collaborative thinking, and strategic prototype planning to prepare teams for the main hackathon.
