Event Overview

Health Hack is a collaborative hackathon jointly organized by VIT Bhopal University and Johns Hopkins University. Taking place from 16-18 February 2026, this three-day innovation marathon includes a pre-workshop on February 16th, followed by the main hackathon on February 17-18. The event focuses on developing accessible healthcare solutions through innovation and collaboration, bringing together bright minds to tackle real-world health challenges.
